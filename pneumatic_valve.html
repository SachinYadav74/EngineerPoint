What are different types of pneumatic valves?

Pneumatic valves are classified on different categories. They are like controlling direction controlling, pressure controlling, flow controlling and shut off valves.

  • Direction Control Valve
  • Pressure Valve
  • Shut off Valve

    • Valve

    Direction Control Valves

    Oct 2

    Direction Control Valve change the flow of direction of compressed air. Depending on their position, actuator movement is dependend. They are mainly classified on basis of

  • Numbers of ports (3/2 way, 5/2way, 4/2 way) first number defines the numbers of ports like 3, 5, 4 here
  • Numbers of positions (3/2 way, 5/3way, 4/2 way) second number defines the numbers of positions like 2, 3, 3 here
  • Actuation method (pushbutton, lever, pneumatic pilot, solenoid)
  • Return Actuation (spring return, double pilot, solenoid)

    Pressure Valve

    Oct 2

    Pressure Valve control the flow of compressed air. They reduce, increase, throttle, monitor, regulate the compressed air flow or pressure.

  • Throttle valve/One way flow control Valve reduce the flow of compressed air in one direction. Video
  • Shuttle Valve/Or gate It has two input for compressed air and one output. When compressed air at any input port is supplied then output is generated. Video
  • Two pressure valve/And gate It has two input for compressed air. To get an output it is compulsory to have input at both the ports. Video
  • Pressure regulating it regulates the pneumatic pressure in circuit. The input pressure is greater than the output pressure.
  • Pressure relief valve it maintains the pressure in circuit. It does not let the pressure increase more than the set limit. If pressure exceeds, the valve opens and extra compressed air is exhausted untill the pressure reduces to the set limit.

    Shut off valve

    Oct 2

    On delay valve has a reservoir, 3/2way normally closed pneumatically operated spring return valve, throttle/one way flow control valve. This throttle valve is adjusted according to the time needed to fill the reservoir. When supplied with compressed air the air gets filled by throttle valve in reservoir. When reservoir is filled the pressure activates 3/2way valve and valve is opened. Video

    Off delay valve has a reservoir, 3/2way normally open pneumatically operated spring return valve, throttle valve. This throttle valve is set according to the time needed to fill the reservoir. When supplied with compressed air, the valve output is availabel as the valve is normally closed.The air gets filled in reservoir. When reservoir is filled the pressure activates 3/2way valve and valve is opened. Video
