Disconnecting

            Before working on any electrical machine one must make sure all the phases are disconnected and all circuit breakers should be awitched off. Remove all powersupply connections, plugs, switch of all buttons, etc. This is most important rule to follow.

Locking

            Once you have disconnected all circuit breakers and fuses make sure you put a cafety lock This will ensure your safety. & Suppose you switchal off MCBs and working. Someone comes and switch on the MCB. This will caus! Severe damage.or even death. Usting lock or tag, this will prevent Anyone else to switch on supply voltage.

Determine absence of Voltage

            Once you put lock, it is your duty to check absence of voltage. Circuits have capacitors, battrie battries. They are charged. They have voltage & Current. It can be dangerous. With multimeter you must check absence of voltage then only handle electrical components.

Earthing

            Suppose there are electrical components with metallic body. If any line of phase wire's insulation is damaged. Then voltage will be supplied to metallic body. If you touch such metallic body then you may get severe shock. If earthing is connected such accidents can be -prevented. Metallic body is connected to earthing so voltage from metallic body flows to earthing terminals.

insulating

            Another method to prevent accidents from metallic body is covering such body with insulating material like plastic, rubber etc. Due to this even though if voltage is present in metallic body you will be safe. You must also use insulating hand gloves, tools that have insulated cover. To protect yourself.
