MCB stands for Miniature circuit breaker. Its main function is to protect circuit by Disconnecting the supply phase. Usually called tripping of MCB. MCB is tripped by 2 main reasons.
    Overload
    Short Circuit

Overload means many devices of higher power rating are connected to same circuit. For example, in our home we start motor, mixer, washing machine and refrigerator at the same time. If the power rating of these machines increase than the MCB capacity, the MCB trips.

Short-Ciruiting means line and neutral are connected without any load or resistance between them. This may happen due to cut on insulation of cable.
