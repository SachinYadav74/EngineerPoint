What are different types of Mechanical Gears?

Oct 23

Power can be transmitted through various mechanical devices. Some mechanical devices like belts, chain, pulleys, sprockets, deifferent types of gears, clutches and universal joints are mostly used machanical assemblies for power transmission.

Gear Drive System

      Gears are used to transmit power. Their teeths are engaged to each other causing . Their driver is a motor connected with coupling and locking pins. The motor transfer power in the form of rotary motion. We need to consider rotation speed, torques and rotation direction while using gear assemblies with motor.

Different types of gears are:

        Spur Gear

        Bevel Gear

        Worm Gear

Spur Gear

      Spur and helical gears are used to transmit power. Spur gears are connected parallel. These gears causes change in rotation direction and depending on gears size torque and rpm changes. These gears can be helical, straight, double helical in types.

Bevel Gear

      These gears are used for transmitting power in 90°. A horizontal drive shaft is connected to vertical drive shaft. These are mainly used in differential of cars. The proppeller shaft drives power from gear box to differential and differential distributes the power to both the wheels.

Worm Gear

      When it comes to large gear ratios worm gear drive can be best choice. There is a shaft with single or double threaded and one gear is connected straight to it. This gear mainly covert high rotational frequency to low and low torque to high.
